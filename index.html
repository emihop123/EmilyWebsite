Bees are currently facing extinction
They're at risk due to climate breakdown and habitat loss.
Shifts in the seasons are disrupting flowering times and the availability of food,
shelter and nesting sites. Climate change also brings extremes of drought,
heavier rainfall and flooding. Bees and pollinating insects are struggling to survive.
